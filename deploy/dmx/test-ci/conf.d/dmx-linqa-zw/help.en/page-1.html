The platform helps with overcoming language barriers by providing shared workspaces. A workspace comprises of both, a whiteboard on which texts, documents, videos, notes, and photos can be placed, and a chat/discussion panel.

The contents of both, the whiteboard, and all chat/discussion messages are translated automatically. So everything happening on the platform is available in German and French instantly.

Use the upper/right button to choose your preferred display language.

There are 3 user roles: users can read/watch whiteboard contents, chat/discuss, and download documents. Editors put content items on the whiteboard and arrange them. Administrators can create workspaces and invite users to them.
