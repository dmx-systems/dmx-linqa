Workspaces

As a user of the Zukunftswerk platform you are a member of one ore more shared workspaces. A workspace is a topic specific area where you communicate and collaborate with the other workspace members.

A workspace is a safe place. Only members of a workspace can see what is going on inside. In the Zukunftswerk platform there are no public workspaces. The Zukunftswerk platform is invitation-only.
Neither anonymous internet users without a Zukunftswerk platform account can see any of the platform content, nor can it be found by internet search engines.

To see the workspaces you're a member of and jump between them use the menu at the top of the screen. Once you jump to a workspace both, its whiteboard, and its chat/discussion panel are displayed immediately.

Workspaces are created and invitations are send out by the platform administrators.
